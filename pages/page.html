Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How can I open a new account with Bank of Maharashtra?

A: You can open an account online via our website or visit the nearest branch with KYC documents.

Q: How to reset my internet banking password?

A: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the internet banking login page or visit a branch.

Q: What are the working hours of Bank of Maharashtra branches?

A: Most branches operate from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM on Saturdays.
